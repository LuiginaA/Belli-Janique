Chiropractic Adjustments

How an Adjustment Works

The chiropractic adjustment represents the fundamental core principle of chiropractic soft tissue disorders and health problems. The chiropractic adjustment represents the fundamental core principle of chiropractic soft tissue disorders and health problems.

The chiropractic adjustment represents the fundamental core principle of the chiropractic treatment.

It is used successfully to treat a variety of different soft tissue disorders as well as a number of other health problems.

The Chiropractic Adjustment or Manipulation is the process of applying a quick but gentle pressure to "subluxated" vertebrae in a corrective manner.

Subluxation exists when a vertebra loses its normal superior and inferior alignment, or when a vertebra loses its normal motion.

The chiropractic adjustment delivers gentle movement into these restricted joints and tight muscles. By encouraging proper motion, pain and inflammation are reduced.

The adjustments are not and should not be painful. In fact, most patients look forward to their chiropractic adjustments.

The adjustments usually provide patients with immediate relief from pain and discomfort, improved mobility and muscle tightness and an increase in one's sense of well being.

FAKTR - Blading

Instrument Assisted

FAKTR stands for Functional and Kinetic Treatment with Rehabilitation. Dr. Janique is certified in this advanced treatment method,

What to Expect During your FAKTR session, you may experience some temporary discomfort as scar tissue is broken down, but this is part of the healing process. Most patients require multiple sessions depending on their condition, with treatment frequency determined during your initial consultation. Recovery between sessions is typically minimal, allowing you to continue with most daily activities.

How FAKTR Differs While based on similar principles to the Graston technique, FAKTR goes beyond traditional instrument-assisted soft tissue mobilization by incorporating functional movement, rehabilitation exercises, and multiple treatment modalities into each session, creating a more comprehensive approach to healing.

Pre and Post-Treatment Care Before your session, wear comfortable clothing that allows easy access to the treatment area. After treatment, staying well-hydrated and following any prescribed exercises will optimize your healing process and help maintain the improvements achieved during your session.

Ready to Start Your Recovery? Contact us today to schedule a consultation and discover if FAKTR is the right solution for your condition. Dr. Janique will assess your specific situation and create a personalized treatment plan to get you back to optimal health.
