Hello, I’m Dr Janique

Your dedicated Chiropractor ...

I earned my Master’s Degree in Chiropractic from the University of Johannesburg, accredited by the European Council of Chiropractic.

Nearing the end of my studies, I actively sought a mentor to guide my professional and personal growth. I was referred to a well-regarded chiropractor in the northern suburbs, who taught me various techniques that greatly enriched my knowledge.

I am registered with the Allied Health Professions Council of South Africa (AHPCSA) and am a member of the Chiropractic Association of South Africa (CASA) and Paediatric Chiropractic South Africa (PCSA).

Mission Statement

With passion and compassion, “My Mission” is in helping others through a positive and mental attitude by providing chiropractic and wellness care to all my patients.
Empowering my patients with inspiration and information needed to choose solutions for themselves and their families.
Maintaining the highest of ethics, integrity, and professional standards today, tomorrow, and for generations to come.

Vision Statement

Everyone deserves the highest quality of a pain free healthy life.
My philosophy is to offer my patients impeccable service in a comfortable environment.
Achieving an understanding and knowledge of the chiropractic treatment they receive, how their body functions and maintaining a healthy life.
